    Increase

    When all work is completed, pain is 0–2, and RPE is 7 or lower: usually add 2.5–5 lb for upper-body exercises or 5–10 lb for lower-body exercises.

    Hold steady

    At RPE 8, or when form was merely acceptable: repeat the same weight and improve control.

    Reduce or modify

    At RPE 9–10, missed repetitions, or pain above 2: reduce the weight or use an easier variation. Sharp or worsening pain should not be trained through.
